Shahed

— Archive for Political Violence

This platform preserves evidence on behalf of victims of political violence. Submissions undergo manual review before their cryptographic hash digest is written to a public blockchain for tamper-proof long-term archival.

Privacy notice: Original files and sensitive testimony are accessible only to authorised reviewers and will never appear in public case queries. Do not submit materials containing high-risk identifying information over unencrypted networks.
